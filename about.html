The Mission

Jarapy Therapy exists to provide high-end emotional support services to office professionals experiencing the stress of light workloads, minor inconveniences, and urgent requests that inevitably become Jared Fox’s responsibility.

Jared Fox

Jared currently serves as a VDC Manager at Walker Mechanical. He is a U.S. Navy veteran who transitioned from field operations into the office, where problems became less physical but significantly more numerous.

Outside of work, Jared enjoys time with his family, though this time is frequently interrupted by “quick questions” that are never quick.

Core Philosophy

  • If you have a problem, Jared will hear about it.
  • If Jared hears about it, Jared will own it.
  • If Jared owns it, it is now urgent.
  • If it is urgent, it is now yesterday’s priority.
