About Jarapy Therapy

Mission

A structured environment for processing workplace concerns through calm intake, guided reflection, and silent assignment to Jared Fox.

Jared Fox

VDC Manager at Walker Mechanical. U.S. Navy veteran. Primary system operator responsible for absorbing organizational complexity.

Core Principle

If a problem is expressed, it enters the system. If it enters the system, it becomes structured. If it becomes structured, it becomes Jared’s.
